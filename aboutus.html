Our story

Origin Music, business concept inspired few years ago by a young man passionate about music, education and creativity.

Initially just an idea, it has grown into a vision to make musical instruments and its services affordable to schools and individuals.

What started as a vision slowly grew into a mission: to make music more accessible to everyone who's willing to learn and grow. In 2023, this vision took shape as Origin Music - a small business with a big heart, dedicated to supporting schools, students, and musicians by offering instruments, accessories, and services at fair prices.

We aim to be more than just a store. Its vision is to become an inspiring and nurturing community where every learner, no matter their background, can feel encouraged to explore, play, and grow through music.
