Our Story

Berin Akida, CEO

Origin Music began few years ago with Berin Akida - young passionate about music and education. What started as a simple idea has grown into a vision: making musical instruments and services affordable to schools and individuals.

We are more than a store - we are a movement to make music education accessible, to encourage creativity, and to support musicians at every level of their journey.

Our vision is to build an inspiring and nurturing community where every person feels comfortable exploring and growing confidently through music.
